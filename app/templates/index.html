Study Schedule Generator

Transform your classes into an organized calendar automatically

What does this application do?

This tool transforms your class list (from Udemy or an Excel spreadsheet) into a calendar file (.ics) that you can import into Google Calendar, Outlook, or any other calendar application.

You define when you want to study, how many hours per day, and the application automatically distributes all your classes across available days, respecting your time limits.

Time Settings

Study Settings
Purpose: Choose which days of the week you are available to study.
How it's used: The application will only schedule classes on the selected days. For example, if you only check Monday to Friday, there will be no classes on weekends.
Select the days you can study
Purpose: Defines how many hours you want to study per day.
How it's used: The application will not schedule more than this limit of hours in a single day. If you set 2 hours, each day will have a maximum of 2 hours of scheduled classes.
Maximum hours you want to study per day
Purpose: Adjusts class duration to give more or less time.
How it's used:
  • 1.0 = normal time (a 30min class stays 30min)
  • 1.5 = 50% more time (30min becomes 45min)
  • 0.8 = 20% less time (30min becomes 24min)
Useful if you watch at different speeds or need time for breaks/notes.
